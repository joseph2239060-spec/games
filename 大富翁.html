About Cute Monopoly

Cute Monopoly is a candy-themed board game for 2 to 4 players on one device.

Roll the dice and move around the board. Buy lands, build houses and hotels to raise the toll, and charge rivals when they land on your property. Pass Start to collect ¥200. When a player can't pay, they go bankrupt — the last player standing wins! Great for family game night — play together on phone, tablet or computer, free and no download.
