About Classic Snakes and Ladders

Classic Snakes and Ladders is a beloved board game for 2 to 4 players, played on a numbered grid from 1 to 100.

How to play: On your turn, roll the die and move your token forward that many squares. The first player to reach square 100 wins.

Ladders and snakes: If you land on the foot of a ladder, climb up to its top. If you land on the head of a snake, slide back down to its tail. One lucky ladder — or one unlucky snake — can change everything.

A game of chance: Snakes and Ladders is driven entirely by the roll of the die, which makes it perfectly fair and a great first board game for young children — no strategy required, just good luck and a little drama.

Did you know? The game comes from ancient India, where it was called Moksha Patam and used to teach how good deeds (ladders) lift you up while bad ones (snakes) bring you down. Play with friends or family on one screen — phone, tablet or computer — free and no download.
