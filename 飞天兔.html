🎯 How to play: tap the screen (or press Space) to make the bunny flap upward. Let go and gravity pulls it down. Fly through the gaps in the candy pipes — each pipe you pass is +1 point. Touch a pipe, the ground, or the sky and the run ends. How far can you fly? 🐰

About Cute Flappy Rabbit

Cute Flappy Rabbit is a free one-tap arcade game. Tap to flap a little bunny through endless candy pipes and chase a new high score.

One control, endless challenge: every tap lifts the bunny, gravity does the rest. Time your taps, slip through each gap, and your score climbs. It is easy to learn, hard to master — and works on phone, tablet and desktop with no download.
